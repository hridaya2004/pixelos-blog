## Applying

If you are confident you meet all the requirements, open a new issue in the [Official Devices repository](https://github.com/PixelOS-AOSP/official_devices/issues/new/choose) and select the **Official Maintainer Form**. Invalid or incomplete submissions will be closed without review.

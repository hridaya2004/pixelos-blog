These guidelines apply to [PixelOS Chat](https://t.me/pixeloschat) and all PixelOS-affiliated Telegram communities.

The PixelOS Telegram community is a place for users to ask questions, share feedback, and discuss everything related to PixelOS. All members are expected to follow the rules below. Violations may result in a warning or removal from the community.

1. **No illegal or pirated content.** Do not post or share links to pirated software, apps, or any other copyrighted material.

2. **No explicit or inappropriate content.** This includes pornography, graphic violence, or anything that may be offensive to other members.

3. **No offensive or discriminatory language.** Racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise hateful language is not tolerated. Occasional casual language is acceptable, but excessive use will not be.

4. **No harassment or bullying.** Personal attacks, insults, or any behavior intended to harm or upset another member is not allowed.

5. **No ETA requests.** Do not ask when the next update or release is coming. Development timelines are unpredictable and we do not provide ETAs.

6. **Communicate in English.** Extended conversations in other languages are not permitted. If English is not your first language, please use a translator.

7. **Stay on topic.** Keep discussions relevant to PixelOS. Off-topic conversations should be taken elsewhere.

8. **No spam or unsolicited promotion.** Do not advertise products or services without prior approval from a moderator.

9. **Appropriate profile pictures only.** Explicit or offensive profile images are not allowed.

10. **Do not misuse the report function.** Only use it to report genuine rule violations.

11. **Do not DM members without permission.** If you have a question, ask it in the appropriate community channel.

12. **No witch-hunting or doxxing.** Do not expose or reveal private information about other members.

13. **Respect moderator and admin decisions.** They are here to keep the community running smoothly. Their decisions are final.

14. **Search before asking.** Check the website and the XDA thread for your device before posting common questions. Do not repeatedly ask the same things.

15. We reserve the right to remove any content or accounts that violate these guidelines.

16. These guidelines may be updated at any time without prior notice.
